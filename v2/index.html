Vestige Production is a student level production committed to building reportoire of high quality videos.

Started by Wayne Ren in the fall of 2006, it's work has come to include short movies, music videos and even full lenght feature presentations. But it wasn't always known as Vestige Productions. First known as "A Wayne's World film" back in the spring of 2003, Wayne's passion of filmaking was sparked by a TV Productions class led by Mr. Rocco Iacovone. Early productions included short films depicting rough choreography in the genre of martial arts entertainment and fantasy. "A Wayne's World film" ended with a feature presentation called Mortal Conflict : The Loud and the Voiceless. This was the last martial art venture which led to more dramatic and comedic productions under what is now known as VESTIGE PRODUCTIONS.

Since it's creation, the dedicated team has been progressively improving. Members of the team include wrtiers, directors, cameramen, editors, producers, public advisors, actors, and much more.

The team was first envisioned by its creator as a way to unite those interested in this field under one name, to offer a channel of creativity throughout the public. Though it is still a student level production, it will hopefully - someday - grow to be so much more.
